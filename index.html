World Cup 2026 Facts

  • 48 Teams: The 2026 tournament is the first in history to feature 48 nations instead of 32.
  • 3 Host Nations: Jointly hosted by the United States (11 cities), Mexico (3 cities), and Canada (2 cities).
  • 104 Matches: Expanded format leads to 104 matches, increasing playing schedules to 39 days.
  • Round of 32: Top two of each group and the eight best third-placed teams qualify for the new knockout stage.
